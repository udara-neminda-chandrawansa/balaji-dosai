Welcome to Balaji Dosai

Discover the Art of Authentic Sri Lankan Cuisine

Discovering the art of local cuisine offers you a chance to explore genuine Sri Lankan tastes and traditional meals. These culinary workshops are crafted to teach you how to prepare beloved Sri Lankan heritage dishes. The process is simple and enjoyable to master. These recipes work well in any home kitchen, requiring only fundamental ingredients and some patience - no complex techniques needed.

Under the guidance of our expert local chefs, you’ll master iconic dishes like hoppers, kottu roti, and creamy coconut curries, using fresh ingredients and time-honored techniques. By the end of the session, you’ll not only enjoy a homemade feast but also take home recipes to recreate Sri Lankan magic in your own kitchen.
