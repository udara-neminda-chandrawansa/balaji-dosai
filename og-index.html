Welcome to Balaji Dosai

Discover the Art of Authentic Sri Lankan Cuisine

Learning how to cook like a local will give you the opportunity to experience authentic Sri Lankan flavors and dishes.This cooking classes are designed to introduce the recipes for making the famous Sri Lankan traditional food. This is very easy and fun to learn. The recipes can be made in any kitchen. They are not complicated and all you will need is the basic ingredients and a little patience

Under the guidance of our expert local chefs, you’ll master iconic dishes like hoppers, kottu roti, and creamy coconut curries, using fresh ingredients and time-honored techniques. By the end of the session, you’ll not only enjoy a homemade feast but also take home recipes to recreate Sri Lankan magic in your own kitchen.

Mr. Upul Rathnayaka

Founder
